RAM LIBRE :

(Get-WmiObject -Class Win32_OperatingSystem).FreePhysicalMemory/1MB

 

Invoke-Command -ComputerName SIDFSLX01 -ScriptBlock {(Get-WmiObject -Class Win32_OperatingSystem).FreePhysicalMemory/1MB}

 

RAM TOTAL :

(Get-WmiObject -Class Win32_ComputerSystem ).TotalPhysicalMemory/1GB

 

Invoke-Command -ComputerName VIDFSAV01 -ScriptBlock {(Get-WmiObject -Class Win32_ComputerSystem ).TotalPhysicalMemory/1GB}

 

 

RAM TOTAL HYPER-V en LOCAL sur l'hyper-v :

Get-VM | measure-object -property memoryassigned -sum | select-object sum/1GB
